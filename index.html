How to Play:

  1. Guess the correct word by selecting one letter at a time. You may click on a letter on the screen or use your keyboard.
  2. If the letter you selected is correct, it will appear on the appropriate dashes.
  3. If the letter selected is incorrect, the stick figure will be one step closer to being hanged!
  4. You have six incorrect guesses before the poor stick figure is hanged and the game is over.
  5. For each word you correctly guess you get one point.
  6. The game ends when you run out of guesses and the stick figure gets hanged!
  7. Click 'New Game' to start over.
